Vitamins

Their importance in life

I always wanted to research and read about vitamins, and i think now it is a good opportunity to do so. What inspired me was a book I read some years ago, called "Vitamin D", by Prof. Dr. Michael F. Holick. In these pages I will try to talk a little about the things I now and interisting facts about vitamins.

Vitamins are organic substances that people need in small quantities. Each one has a different role in the body, and they are essential in your health. They are generally classified in fat soluble or water soluble, based in where they dissolve.
