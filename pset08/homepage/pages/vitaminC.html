Vitamin C

Healer

Vitamin C, also called ascorbic acid, is a water-soluvle vitamin that is not well stored, so it must be taken daily. It helps controlling infections and healing wounds, being also a antioxidant that can neutralize harmful free radicals. It is needed make collagen, hormones and chemical messengers. Estimulates white blood cells.

Interisting enough, megadoses of this vitamin is generally not considered toxic, because the body can not absorb this vitamin well, it's absorption decreases once body's tissues become saturated with vitamin C and any excess will be excreted in urine. But overdose can still happen, causing diarrhea, increased formation of kidney stones and level of uric acid.

Fruits and vegetables are the best sources of this vitamin, mainly citrus fruits(oranges, kiwi, lemon, grapefruit) and cruciferous vegetables (broccoli, Brussels sprouts, cabbage, cauliflower), in addition too bell peppers, starwberries, tomatoes and white potatoes.
